# Company Role Requirements (Campus Placement)

## TCS / Infosys — Software Engineer
Required: Java, Python, SQL, DSA, OOP, DBMS, communication, aptitude
GD topics: teamwork, ethics in IT, digital India, AI impact on jobs
Interview: coding (arrays, strings), project explanation, HR (strengths/weaknesses)

## Deloitte / EY — Business Analyst
Required: Excel, SQL, Power BI, statistics, communication, case study, MBA finance basics
GD topics: startup ecosystem, GST, work-life balance, leadership
Interview: guesstimates, resume walkthrough, situational HR

## Goldman / JP Morgan — Finance Analyst (Mkt&Fin)
Required: Excel, valuation basics, accounting, financial modeling, communication, aptitude, internships
GD topics: market volatility, crypto regulation, RBI policy
Interview: technical finance Qs, brain teasers, fit questions

## Amazon / Flipkart — Product Analyst
Required: SQL, Excel, product sense, metrics (DAU, conversion), A/B testing basics, storytelling
GD topics: customer obsession, sustainability, e-commerce growth
Interview: case studies, metric definition, behavioral STAR

## General MBA Placement
Required: communication, leadership, domain knowledge, internship, certifications, LinkedIn-ready resume
GD: current affairs, group coordination, listening skills
PI: Why MBA, why company, career goals, resume deep-dive
