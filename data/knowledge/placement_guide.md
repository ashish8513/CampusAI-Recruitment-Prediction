# Campus Placement & Salary Guide (RAG Knowledge Base)

## Dataset Overview
- 215 MBA student records with academics, work experience, and placement outcomes.
- Target: salary (INR) for placed candidates; placement status (Placed / Not Placed).

## Key Salary Drivers
1. **MBA percentage (mba_p)** — Strongest correlate with package; aim for 65%+.
2. **E-test score (etest_p)** — Campus aptitude test; 75+ improves shortlist odds.
3. **Work experience** — Prior internship/full-time experience often lifts offers 15–25%.
4. **Degree type Sci&Tech** — Tech-heavy profiles align with analytics/product roles.
5. **Specialisation Mkt&Fin** — Finance/marketing roles show higher average packages in this cohort.

## Placement Tips
- Maintain consistent academics: SSC 60%+, HSC 60%+, Degree 60%+.
- Central board (SSC/HSC) students in dataset show mixed outcomes; focus on percentile and projects.
- Commerce/Science stream at HSC: choose degree stream aligned with target industry.
- Status Placed correlates with non-zero salary prediction — improve soft skills, mock GD/PI.

## Typical Salary Ranges (INR, from training data)
- Entry: 2.0 – 3.5 LPA
- Mid: 3.5 – 6.0 LPA  
- Top decile: 6.0 – 10.0+ LPA

## Interview Preparation
- Quantitative aptitude, case studies for Mkt&Fin, HR scenarios for Mkt&HR.
- Highlight leadership in college societies and live projects.

## AI Career Agent Role
Provide actionable, ethical career advice for Indian campus recruitment. Never guarantee placement; cite data-driven ranges.
